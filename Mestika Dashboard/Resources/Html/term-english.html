Anti Fraud

Based on Financial Services Authority Regulation Number 39 / POJK.03 / 2019 concerning Implementation of Anti-Fraud Strategies for Commercial Banks, Bank Mestika, especially Anti Fraud Management, presents informative videos for all Bank Mestika stakeholders in order to raise awareness of the risks of fraud and support each other in eradicating actions Fraud in the banking environment. Have a good time watching.

In our efforts to serve and protect customers from misuse of the Bank's facilities so as to cause losses to customers, or other stakeholders, we have prepared a number of tips so that Bank customers can foster a culture of prudence and vigilance in every banking activity.

Fraud is an act of irregularity or deliberate omission to deceive, deceive, or manipulate a Bank, customer, or other party, which occurs in the internal or external environment of the Bank and / or uses the Bank's facilities so as to cause the Bank, customer, or other party to suffer losses and / or the perpetrator of fraud obtains financial benefits, either directly or indirectly.

The types of actions that are classified as fraud are falsification, embezzlement of assets, banking crimes, theft of information data, and other actions that can be equated with it.

Some tips for you are as follows:

  1. Maintain your savings book, ATM card and deposit slip, check / giro, SDB key in a safe place and do not leave it to other people, even bank officers.
  2. Make sure that each check sheet and bilyet giro in the book you take are complete and in order. (One book contains 25 checks / BG with sequential numbers, and is equipped with a receipt as proof of acceptance and a new book request form)
  3. Immediately report it to the Bank if your check / demand deposit is lost or stolen.
  4. When writing checks / giro, always use indelible ink, typewriters or printers - don't use pencils.
  5. Make sure you write numbers and counts on the check / giro without leaving any spaces and use line strokes to fill in the gaps that arise, which allow additions / changes.
  6. Ensure the correctness of each of your transactions by checking the balance in the passbook and proof of validated transactions, or other banking activities, before you leave the Bank.
  7. Do not authorize people you do not know well and / or Bank employees to transact with the Bank.
  8. Keep proof of receipt of collateral when you hand over the collateral to the Bank.
  9. Always check and match all transactions on your accounts periodically.
  10. Ask and clearly understand any questions, data and documents requested by Bank officers.
  11. Never give your PIN / password to anyone including Bank employees, Use the same PIN / password to enter various websites, write your PIN / password on paper.
  12. Change your PIN / password regularly.
  13. Use a PIN / password that is different from your address, name, telephone number, identity card or date of birth, and avoid using a PIN / password that is easy to guess.
  14. Be careful when using your ATM card and PIN so that it is not seen or read by other people when making transactions at ATMs

  15. Our employees are prohibited from asking for or receiving a reward, commission, money or valuables for services or services provided to customers and / or stakeholders.
  16. Bank employees are required to protect confidential customer information and are prohibited from providing customer data information to other parties without the approval of the Bank Management.

    • The data, information and identity that I have provided in this application form are known and / or suspected to be untrue / inappropriate / false.
